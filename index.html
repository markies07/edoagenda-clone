Edo Agenda Web

In the office, at home or in a café bar, open Edo Agenda on your laptop and take your time to plan your day and free your creative soul.

Edo Agenda Mobile

The best ideas often come on the go. If you're travelling for work or if you're just enjoying your long-awaited trip, use Edo Agenda to write a list of things to do or simply as a journal.

Edo Agenda Paper

Where tradition meets technology. Your traditional everyday note-taking device has a very digital soul. Agenda Paper is a beautifully crafted notebook. By snapping pictures of pages, your ideas transfer to the web. Your tasks, as well as your sketches, designs and thoughts become immediately accessible on the app and safely stored in the cloud. All it takes is a snap.
